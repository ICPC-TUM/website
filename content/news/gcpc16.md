#GCPC 2016 is happening today!

GCPC 2016 is happening today! Over 90 teams representing 16 universities all over Germany registered to compete at one of 13 contest sides. An overview of the contest sides can be found [here](https://icpc.tum.de/contests/gcpc).
Once the contest starts you can find the scoreboard [here](https://judge.in.tum.de/gcpc/public/scoreboard.php). We will post pictures on our [Facebook page](https://facebok.com/icpctum) so make sure to visit us there as well. 

Earlier this week, from Wednesday until Friday, the practice session took place containing three problems to warm up for today's main event. The problemset can be found [here](https://icpc.tum.de/content/contests/history/2016/gcpc/practicesession.pdf). The session was won by TUM's own oachkatzschwoaf - congratulations!
Solution slides for the practice session can be found [here](https://icpc.tum.de/content/contests/history/2016/gcpc/practicesession_solutions.pdf). Also, we made a video explaining the solutions to all problems which can be found [here](https://www.facebook.com/IcpcTum/videos/800721756729972/).
